About us

Riding on the power bank sharing technology, Baison is committed to fostering an offline platform integrating comprehensive services and marketing for new retail.
Established in Mar. 2016, Baison DMCC is a high-tech IT company engaged in the rental and sharing services of intelligent charging devices. Responding to the call of the Belt and Road Initiative, Baison opened the Middle East market in 2019 and offered all-round technological items and services for China-invested enterprises under the guidance of Go Out Policy by virtue of Dubai's regional advantage of being the center of international trade and finance.
Baison sticks to establishing loyal partnership with customers on the basis of efficient operations, sophisticated technologies, premium products and excelsior spirits.
